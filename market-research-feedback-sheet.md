# KnowYourRole - User Feedback Sheet

**What is KnowYourRole?**
KnowYourRole is a free personality discovery app that combines three science-backed assessments (Myers-Briggs, Big Five, and DISC) into one interactive quiz. Based on your answers, it generates personalized results including personality breakdowns, career matches, and growth insights.

**Instructions:** Visit **knowyourole.com** on your phone or laptop. Go through the full experience from start to finish (select your age tier, pick a mood, take the quiz, and explore your results).
