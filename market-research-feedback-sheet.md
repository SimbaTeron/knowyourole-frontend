# KnowYourRole - User Feedback Sheet

**What is it?** KnowYourRole is a free personality quiz that combines three assessments (Myers-Briggs, Big Five, and DISC) to give you personalized results, career matches, and growth insights.

**Instructions:** Visit **knowyourole.com** on your phone or laptop. Go through the full experience from start to finish, then answer the questions below honestly. Short answers are perfectly fine.

---

**1. Quiz Engagement** -- Did the quiz hold your attention? Was the length (35 questions) about right? Did the timer help or stress you out?

&nbsp;

**2. Onboarding Flow** -- Before the quiz you pick a mood, mix moods, enter a location, and see a demo. Did these steps feel like a fun warm-up or unnecessary hurdles?

&nbsp;

**3. Results Usefulness** -- Did your results (MBTI type, Big Five traits, DISC style) teach you something new about yourself? Would you use them for anything like career planning or self-reflection?

&nbsp;

**4. Charts and Visuals** -- The results include a radar chart, colored bar chart, and percentile breakdowns. Were they easy to understand? Did they help more than text alone?

&nbsp;

**5. Career Matches** -- You received career suggestions with match percentages. Did the suggested roles feel realistic and relevant to you right now? Were the explanations helpful?

&nbsp;

**6. Premium Insights** -- After the main results there are extra insights (side hustles, blindspots, growth tips, dream role advisor). Did you explore them? Were any particularly useful?

&nbsp;

**7. Account Creation / Login** -- Were you prompted to sign in or create an account? How did it feel -- smooth, confusing, or pushy? Did it feel truly optional?

&nbsp;

**8. Look and Feel** -- What did you think of the app's visual design, colors, and overall tone? Did it feel trustworthy and polished, or did anything look off or cheap?

&nbsp;

**9. Mobile Experience** -- If on your phone: were swipe gestures smooth? Was anything hard to tap or read? If on a laptop: did anything feel awkward?

&nbsp;

**10. Overall Value** -- Was this experience worth your time? Would you recommend it to a friend or share your results? What one thing would make you come back?

&nbsp;

---
*Thank you for your honest feedback!*
